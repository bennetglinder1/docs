---
title: "get_company"
description: "Full company profile — org details, all contacts at the account, and company-level facts"
---

Returns everything known about a company: firmographic data, every contact at that account with their pipeline stage and ICP score, and all company-level facts. Use this for account-based work — multi-stakeholder deals, renewal prep, or any time you need the full picture of an org.

## Parameters

| Name | Type | Required | Description |
|------|------|----------|-------------|
| `company_id` | string | Yes | Company UUID — available as `company_id` on any contact |

## Returns

Formatted text block containing:

- **Header**: company name and domain
- **Firmographics**: industry, employee count, deal health score
- **Contacts**: everyone at the account — name, title, stage, and `contact_id`
- **Company facts**: stored facts shared across the whole org

```
Acme Corp (acmecorp.com)
Industry: SaaS  |  Size: 250  |  Health: 74

Contacts (2 of 2):
  Alex Chen · VP of Engineering — evaluating  (a1b2c3d4-...)
  Sarah Kim · CFO — aware  (c3d4e5f6-...)

Company facts:
  [deal] Evaluating 3 vendors, decision by end of Q2 · 3d ago
  [deal] Budget confirmed at $24k ARR · 12d ago
  [risk] Security review required before sign-off · 1mo ago

(company_id: b2c3d4e5-...)
```

## When to call

Call `get_company` when you need the full account view — who the stakeholders are, where each person is in the funnel, and what's been learned about the org. Then use `get_contact` on the individual you're about to act on.

<Tip>
Get `company_id` from any contact's profile — it's always included in `get_contact` output.
</Tip>
