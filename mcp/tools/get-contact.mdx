title: "get_contact"
description: "Get everything known about a contact — the one tool to call before acting on any person"
---

Returns the full profile for a contact: identity, pipeline stage, a live **status line** (are you waiting on them or are they waiting on you?), AI-generated summary, recent activities with direction arrows, and stored facts. This is the primary lookup tool — call it before writing an email, preparing for a call, or making any decision about a contact.

## Parameters

| Name | Type | Required | Description |
|------|------|----------|-------------|
| `email` | string | One of | Contact's email address |
| `contact_id` | string | One of | Contact UUID — use if you already have it |

Either `email` or `contact_id` must be provided.

## Returns

Formatted text block containing:

- **Header**: name · title · company
- **Scores**: pipeline stage, ICP score, deal health score
- **Status**: live-derived direction — who needs to act next and how long ago the last touch was
- **Summary**: AI-generated 2-sentence relationship summary
- **Last 7 days**: full activity body with direction arrows (`←` inbound / `→` outbound)
- **7–30 days**: top 3 activities by signal weight (proposal > call > email > LinkedIn > note)
- **History (30–90d)**: counts by activity type only — no body
- **Facts**: up to 5 stored facts with relative age (`3d ago`, `2mo ago`); overflow shown as `(+N more)`
- **Company facts**: facts shared across everyone at the company
- Footer with `contact_id` and `company_id`

## Direction arrows

Activities are annotated with direction based on source and activity type:

| Arrow | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| `→` | You reached out — email sent, proposal, follow-up, agent-logged activity |
| `←` | They reached out — inbound webhook, LinkedIn reply, website visit |
| `↔` | Direction unknown |

The **Status** line is derived from the most recent activity. `← Waiting on your reply` means the last touch was inbound — the ball is in your court.

## Activity tiers

Activities are compressed into three temporal tiers to keep the output token-efficient:

| Tier | Window | What you see |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Hot | 0–7 days | Full message body + direction arrow |
| Warm | 7–30 days | Top 3 by signal weight + direction arrow |
| History | 30–90 days | Counts by type only |

Signal weight order: `proposal_sent` (10) › `call_held` (8) › `email` (6) › `linkedin_message` (4) › `manual_note` (2).

## When to call

Call `get_contact` at the start of any task involving a specific person. It gives the agent everything needed to act — status, history, and stored facts — in a single call. Also call `get_memories` before drafting outreach so you have your own ICP and product context alongside the contact's profile.

<Tip>
The underlying REST endpoint is `GET /v1/contacts/:id`. The MCP tool formats the raw data into a single text block optimised for agent prompts.
</Tip>
