## What is Proply?

Proply is **GTM data infrastructure for agents**.

Your GTM stack is fragmented — Apollo for prospecting, Salesforce for CRM, Smartlead for sequences, Gmail for email, LinkedIn for social. Each tool has part of the picture. None of them talk to each other in real time, and none of them are queryable by an AI agent.

Proply fixes that. It unifies every signal across your outbound, communication, and CRM tools into **one identity-resolved record per human** — then exposes the full account context to your agents through a single MCP call.

Your agent knows everything before it acts. Every touchpoint. Every signal. Every deal stage.

## What it does

- **Unified identity resolution** — No duplicates, no gaps. Every contact across Apollo, Salesforce, Smartlead, Gmail, and LinkedIn is merged into one clean record.
- **Complete timeline visibility** — Every email, call, LinkedIn message, and CRM event in one place. Know exactly where a prospect stands before acting.
- **Live sync** — Outbound tools, communication tools, and CRM stay perfectly in sync. One update propagates everywhere.
- **Agent-ready context** — Agents query the full account context in a single MCP call. No data wrangling.
- **Revenue attribution** — Tie campaigns, channels, and actions directly to pipeline and closed deals.

## Who it's for

- **Outbound agencies** running 10–50 client stacks
- **GTM engineers** at outbound-heavy companies, owning the agent data layer
- **RevOps leads** owning data quality, deduplication, and attribution
