## What is Proply?

Proply is **account memory for AI agents**. It gives your agents a persistent, structured understanding of every relationship — who the contact is, what they care about, where they are in the pipeline, and what signals have been observed.

Most CRMs are built for humans clicking through a UI. Proply is built for agents reading and writing context programmatically — through an MCP server your agent calls directly, or a REST API you can wire into any workflow.

- **People memory** — Private facts about individuals: communication style, preferences, and key context from past interactions
- **Company memory** — Org-level facts shared across every contact at that company
- **Pipeline signals** — Activity events from email, LinkedIn, calendar, webhooks, and third-party sources
- **AI synthesis** — Proply reads the raw signal stream and writes structured summaries back into memory automatically
