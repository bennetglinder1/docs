title: HubSpot
description: Connect HubSpot to sync contacts and deals into Proply.
---

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Private Apps in HubSpot">
    In HubSpot go to **Settings → Integrations → Private Apps** and click **Create a private app**.

    <img style={{width:"60%"}} src="/images/providers/hubspot/step-1-private-apps.png" alt="Open Private Apps in HubSpot" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name the app and select scopes">
    Give the app a name (e.g. "Proply") then go to the **Scopes** tab and enable:

    ```
    crm.objects.contacts.read
    crm.objects.deals.read
    crm.objects.companies.read
    ```

    <img style={{width:"60%"}} src="/images/providers/hubspot/step-2-scopes.png" alt="Select HubSpot scopes" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create and copy the access token">
    Click **Create app** then copy the **Access token** shown.

    <img style={{width:"60%"}} src="/images/providers/hubspot/step-3-access-token.png" alt="Copy HubSpot access token" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect in Proply">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations → + Add Integration → HubSpot**, paste the token, and click **Connect**.

    <img style={{width:"60%"}} src="/images/providers/hubspot/step-4-connect.png" alt="Connect HubSpot in Proply" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
