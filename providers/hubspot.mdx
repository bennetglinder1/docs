---
title: "HubSpot"
description: "Import contacts, companies, and deals from HubSpot"
---

HubSpot is a one-time bootstrap import. Proply pulls your existing contacts, companies, and deals and merges them into the unified record. After import, Proply operates independently — there is no ongoing sync back to HubSpot.

## What gets imported

* Contacts — name, email, company, phone, HubSpot owner
* Companies — name, domain, industry, location
* Deals — name, value, stage, close date

## Step 1: Connect HubSpot

1. Open Proply → **Settings → Integrations → CRM**
2. Click **Connect HubSpot**
3. Complete the HubSpot OAuth flow — you will be redirected back automatically
4. The connection is saved encrypted to your workspace

## Step 2: Run the import

1. After connecting, click **Import from HubSpot**
2. Select which object types to import (contacts, companies, deals)
3. Optionally search for specific records or import all
4. Click **Import** — Proply runs identity resolution and merges duplicates automatically

## Step 3: Verify

Open **Contacts** in Proply. Your HubSpot contacts should appear with their company and deal associations intact.

<Note>
  Importing the same contacts twice is safe — Proply deduplicates on email and HubSpot ID. Existing records are updated, not duplicated.
</Note>
