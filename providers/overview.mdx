---
title: "Providers"
description: "Connect your GTM stack to Proply"
---

Proply connects to your existing GTM tools to ingest relationship signals. Every event — email sent, LinkedIn message, meeting held, website visit — flows into the unified contact record automatically.

<Note>
  Providers are configured per workspace. Add your keys under **Settings → Integrations** in the app.
</Note>

## Signal providers

Ingest real-time signals from your communication and outbound tools.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin" href="/providers/linkedin">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Calendar" icon="calendar" href="/providers/google-calendar">
    Meetings, calls, rescheduled events, and no-shows.
  </Card>
