---
title: "Providers"
description: "Connect your GTM stack to Proply"
---

Proply connects to your existing GTM tools to ingest relationship signals. Every event — email sent, LinkedIn message, meeting held, website visit — flows into the unified contact record automatically.

<Note>
  All providers are connected per workspace under **Settings → Integrations** in the app.
</Note>

## Signal providers

Real-time signals from your communication, outbound, and meeting tools.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin" href="/providers/linkedin">
    Connection requests, messages, and profile activity via Unipile OAuth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Calendar" icon="calendar" href="/providers/google-calendar">
    Meetings, calls, rescheduled events, and no-shows.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fireflies.ai" icon="microphone" href="/providers/fireflies">
    Meeting transcripts pushed automatically after every call.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RB2B" icon="eye" href="/providers/rb2b">
    De-anonymised website visitors matched to contacts in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fathom" icon="video" href="/providers/fathom">
    Call recordings and meeting summaries pushed via webhook.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Calendly" icon="calendar-check" href="/providers/calendly">
    Booking created, rescheduled, and cancelled events.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## CRM import

One-time bootstrap import from your existing CRM. Contacts are merged and deduplicated into Proply.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="HubSpot" icon="h" href="/providers/hubspot">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Pipedrive" icon="chart-gantt" href="/providers/pipedrive">
    Import persons, organizations, and deals from Pipedrive.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Airtable" icon="table" href="/providers/airtable">
