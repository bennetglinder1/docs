---
title: "Fathom"
description: "Push call summaries and action items into contact timelines"
---

Fathom records and summarises your calls, then sends a webhook to Proply after each meeting. The call summary and participant emails are logged to the matching contact's timeline.

## What gets ingested

* Meeting title and date
* Participant emails (matched to contacts)
* Fathom AI summary added to contact memory

## Step 1: Get your webhook URL

Open Proply → **Settings → Integrations → Fathom** → copy your webhook URL.

Format: `https://api.yourdomain.com/inbound/fathom?workspace_id=<your-id>`

## Step 2: Add the webhook in Fathom

1. Go to [fathom.video](https://fathom.video) → **Settings → Integrations → Webhooks**
2. Add a new webhook endpoint
3. Paste your Proply URL
4. Select trigger: **Meeting Ended**
5. Save

## Step 3: Verify

Run a test call with Fathom recording. The meeting should appear in the participant's contact timeline in Proply within a few minutes.

<Note>
  Like Fireflies, Fathom webhooks only update existing contacts — they do not create new ones.
</Note>
