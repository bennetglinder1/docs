---
title: "Calendly"
description: "Log booking, rescheduled, and cancelled events to contact timelines"
---

Calendly sends a webhook when a meeting is booked, rescheduled, or cancelled. Proply matches the invitee email to a contact and logs the event to their timeline.

## What gets ingested

* Booking created → `meeting_booked` signal
* Reschedule → `meeting_rescheduled` signal
* Cancellation → `meeting_cancelled` signal
* Invitee name, email, and event type

## Step 1: Get your webhook URL

Open Proply → **Settings → Integrations → Calendly** → copy your webhook URL.

Format: `https://api.yourdomain.com/inbound/calendly?workspace_id=<your-id>`

## Step 2: Add the webhook in Calendly

1. Go to [calendly.com](https://calendly.com) → **Integrations → Webhooks**
2. Click **New Webhook**
3. Paste your Proply webhook URL
4. Select events: **invitee.created**, **invitee.canceled**
5. Save

<Note>
  Calendly webhooks are available on Calendly Professional and above.
</Note>

## Step 3: Verify

Book a test meeting via your Calendly link. The booking should appear as an activity on the contact's timeline in Proply within seconds.
