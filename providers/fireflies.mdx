---
title: "Fireflies.ai"
description: "Push meeting transcripts and summaries into Proply automatically"
---

Fireflies sends a webhook after every recorded meeting. Proply receives it, matches the participants to contacts, and logs the meeting with a summary to the activity timeline.

## What gets ingested

* Meeting title and date
* Participant emails (matched to contacts)
* AI-generated summary pushed to contact memory

## Step 1: Get your webhook URL

Open Proply → **Settings → Integrations → Fireflies** → copy your webhook URL.

Format: `https://api.yourdomain.com/inbound/fireflies?workspace_id=<your-id>`

## Step 2: Add the webhook in Fireflies

1. Go to [app.fireflies.ai](https://app.fireflies.ai) → **Integrations → Webhooks**
2. Click **Add Webhook**
3. Paste your Proply webhook URL
4. Select trigger: **Meeting Completed**
5. Click **Save**

## Step 3: Verify

Record a test meeting with Fireflies. After it processes, the meeting should appear in the contact's timeline in Proply within a few minutes.

<Note>
  Fireflies only sends webhooks for meetings where at least one participant email matches a contact in your Proply workspace. New contacts are not created automatically from Fireflies.
</Note>
